How much to lift?

Your progress is primarily determined by the interaction of three key components: the weight you lift, the number of reps (the times you lift without a break), and the number of sets (the total cycles, e.g., 10 reps). To better progress, choose these three variables to push yourself close to failure while avoiding any unsafe practices. Aim for a rep range of 3-20 and a set range of 2-5. Consistently increase one of these three components, and you'll observe significant progress.
